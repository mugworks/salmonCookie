Amazing Cookies!

Pat's cookies, made from all organic ingredients, are melt-in-your-mouth good. Pat finds the freshest salmon, practically snatching it from the jaws of the grizzlies, and smokes them for days in her specially designed smoker. The smoked salmon is ground into flour, combined with melted butter, sugar, eggs and her special secret ingredient to bring you the yummiest cookies in the northwest. Try some today!
